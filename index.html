User Guide

This is a visualization tool to analyze the daily routines of employees at GAStech. The visualization showcases GPS data from employee car rides and transactional data. Use the filters at the top to add a car ID you are interested in learning more about, and use the range slider to choose what days you are interested in. Adding a car will automatically overlay that car's GPS data to the map within the dates specified by the range slider. To avoid latency issues, the map will not immediately update the range slider is changed. Rather, you must click on the "Update Dates" button to apply a new time range. Cars you have selected will appear in the column to the left of the map in the form of buttons. You can click on a button to remove that car's GPS data from the map. Paths are color coded by the car's ID.

Example Use Cases

  • To see all GPS data, simply remove all selected cars (you can see which ones you've selected in the column to the left of the map), and set the range sliders as far apart as possible and click "Update Dates".
  • To see all GPS data of a certain car, type a car ID into the "Car ID" input form, and press add.
  • To see all GPS data on certain days, just move the range sliders around and click "Update Dates".
  • Combine these filters to look at more specific information!
