User Guide

This is a visualization tool to analyze the daily routines of employees at GAStech. The visualization showcases GPS data from employee car rides (on the map) and transactional data (to the right of the map). Use the filters at the top to add a car ID you are interested in learning more about, and use the range slider to choose what days you are interested in. Adding a car will automatically overlay that car's GPS data to the map within the dates specified by the range slider. To avoid latency issues, the map will not immediately update when the range slider is changed. Rather, you must click on the "Update Dates" button to apply a new time range. Cars you have selected will appear in the column to the left of the map in the form of buttons. You can click on a button to remove that car's GPS data from the map, or click "Remove All" underneath the car id input to remove all cars selected. The transaction table will also immediately respond to changes in the car ids and dates chosen. Paths and rows in the transaction table are color coded by the car's ID.

Note: If you have not selected any cars (the column to the left of the map is empty), then selecting a date range will automatically populate the column to the left with the car ids in that date range.

How To Use the Features and Helpful Tips

  • Valid car ids range from 1-35.
  • To see all GPS data of a certain car, type a car ID into the "Car ID" input form, and press add.
  • To see all GPS data on certain days, just move the range sliders around and click "Update Dates".
  • While no car ids are selected, clicking "Update Dates" will automatically populate the "Cars Selected" column with the cars that drove in the selected date range
  • If you're interested in what cars drove on certain days, then remove all cars, chose your desired date range, and click "Update Dates". This will automatically select all cars that drove on that day, and plot their coordinates on the map.
  • Combine these filters to look at more specific information!
